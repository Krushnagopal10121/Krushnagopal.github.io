Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the grievance redressal process

How long does it take to resolve a grievance?

Most grievances are resolved within 3-5 working days. Complex issues may take up to 10 working days. You'll receive regular updates on the status of your grievance.

Can I track the status of my submitted grievance?

Yes, you can track your grievance status using the token ID provided after submission. Simply visit the "Track Grievance" section and enter your token ID.

What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?

If you're not satisfied with the resolution, you can request escalation to higher authorities through the portal. Your grievance will then be reviewed by the Grievance Redressal Committee.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, all grievances are treated with strict confidentiality. Your personal information is protected as per university policies and data privacy regulations.

Can I submit anonymous grievances?

While we encourage providing contact information for better communication, you can choose to submit anonymous grievances for sensitive matters. However, this may limit our ability to follow up.
